Welcome to RESTAURANT MANAGER

This is the right solution for restaurant owners and managers to efficiently manage their daily operations. Our web application was developed with the goal of streamlining the workflow and making it more organized for your restaurant.
Our Story

At the heart of this project is a real-world need identified by a business owner who was seeking a tool to manage their restaurant's activity flow. Through collaborative brainstorming and idea sketching, we developed a robust yet uncomplicated application that serves the needs of both employees and managers alike.

A look into our role-based access control system

Our application features a robust role-based access control system that enables various levels of user permissions.

With different levels of access, users can perform actions ranging from basic tasks to more advanced actions:

  1. The system administrator has the highest level of access, allowing them to manage users.
  2. The restaurant managers have intermediate access to manage various aspects of the restaurant's workflow.
  3. Employees have basic access, which allows them to read relevant information and complete predefined forms.
Web application

Our web application can be accessed easily from any smartphone, tablet, or laptop, making it accessible to all employees. To access the dashboard, simply login to the application.
